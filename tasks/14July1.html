Delicious Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot.
  2. Add pasta and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  4. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Add diced tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Drain pasta and add it to the sauce. Mix well.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.
